Security Policy

Our Commitment to Security

At MyTube.space, we prioritize the security and privacy of our users. Our security policy outlines our commitment to protecting your information and ensuring a safe browsing experience.

Data Protection

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Secure Browsing: Our website uses secure protocols to protect your browsing activity.
  • No Data Storage: We do not store any personal user data or video content on our servers.
  • Third-party Integration: We only integrate with reputable platforms (YouTube and Dailymotion) using their official APIs.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct periodic security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

Privacy Guidelines

Our privacy measures include:

  • No User Tracking: We do not track or store user search history.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect essential information required for the service to function.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not share any user information with third parties.
  • Transparent Practices: We maintain clear communication about our security practices.

Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in MyTube.space, please follow these steps:

  1. Do not disclose the issue publicly until we have had a chance to address it.
  2. Contact us privately at kuyarod1190@gmail.com with detailed information about the vulnerability.
  3. Provide steps to reproduce the issue, potential impacts, and any recommendations for a fix if possible.
  4. We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and work on resolving the issue promptly.

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will credit researchers who help us improve security.

Supported Versions

We provide security updates for the following versions:

Version Supported Security Updates
Latest (2.0+) ✅ Yes Regular updates
Legacy (1.x) ❌ No End of support

Users are strongly encouraged to use the latest version for optimal security.

Security Update Process

  • Regular Updates: Security patches are released monthly or as needed.
  • Critical Fixes: High-priority vulnerabilities are addressed within 24 hours.
  • Notification: Users will be notified of important security updates through our platform.
  • Verification: All security patches undergo thorough testing before release.

Best Practices for Users

  • Stay Updated: Always use the latest version of MyTube.space.
  • Report Issues: Report any suspicious activity or security concerns immediately.
  • Safe Browsing: Be cautious when clicking on external links.
  • Account Security: If using third-party services, ensure your accounts are properly secured.

Contact Information

For security-related inquiries or to report vulnerabilities, please contact us at kuyarod1190@gmail.com. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and maintaining the security of our platform.
